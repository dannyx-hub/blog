Why Spartan?

2023-07-02 — Written by dannyx-hub

While working on various machines on HTB or playing around with Kali Linux out of curiosity, you have surely come across a tool called nmap. It is a highly sophisticated port scanner, often considered the foundation of any good reconnaissance. It allows for scripting to automate tasks and is quite efficient in its usage.

One might wonder how complex it is to create such an advanced tool. I asked myself that question and created Spartan :)

Spartan is all about convenience, speed, and versatility. It is written in Python and currently works on version 3.10 (there are some issues with 3.11 due to changes in asyncio, but I’m working on it!). It provides optimal speed and supports the creation of custom scripts. Additionally, writing scripts is much more enjoyable since they are written in Python. It also includes service and CMS detection, making the scanning process even more efficient. The tool is open source, and I highly encourage its development. Here on the blog, you will definitely find posts that serve as a developer’s diary of the tool’s progress.
